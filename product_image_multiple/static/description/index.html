Need to add more Pictures for Product ?

Overview

Single image can only uploaded for each products in existing odoo system. This module allows you to upload multiple images and youtube demo of your product.

Allows to upload multiple images in the form of products (product.template).

Allows to add youtube demos of your products

You will get the Video URL by right clicking on youtube. You can Paste that.

Icons in Kanban to view the pictures and video
